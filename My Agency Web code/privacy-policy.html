1 Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us when you use our website, fill out contact forms, or engage with our services. This may include:

  • Personal Identification: Your full name, email address, phone number, and business name
  • Project Information: Project descriptions, budget ranges, expected launch dates, and service requirements
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, and attachments you send us
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited (collected automatically)
  • Cookie Data: Preferences and session data stored via browser cookies

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial details, government ID numbers, or health information through our website.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your project inquiries and provide quotes
  • To deliver the digital services you've contracted us for
  • To send project updates, status reports, and relevant communications
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To send occasional marketing communications (with your consent)
  • To fulfill legal obligations and protect our business interests
  • To analyze website usage patterns and optimize performance

We will never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3 Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share it only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors (e.g., hosting, email, payment processors) who assist in operating our business, subject to confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by applicable law, court order, or government authority
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets
  • Protection of Rights: When necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of YashTech Labs, our clients, or others

4 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data is stored in secure, encrypted Firebase Firestore databases (Google Cloud infrastructure)
  • Our admin panel is password-protected with SHA-256 hashing and session management
  • All website communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized team members only
  • Regular security reviews and updates to our systems

While we implement robust security practices, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords when communicating with us.

5 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (theme preferences, session state)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (anonymized data)

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling certain cookies may affect some website features such as dark/light mode preferences.

We do not use cookies to track your activity across other websites or for targeted advertising.

6 Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Database and hosting services — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Typography delivery — subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • WhatsApp: Optional communication channel — subject to Meta's Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where consent was the legal basis

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@yashtechlabs.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8 Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete such information from our systems.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify clients via email for significant changes

Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
